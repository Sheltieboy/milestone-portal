Group Sessions
First — set your school
Pupils need to be linked to your school so only you and any colleagues at that school can see them. Pick your school below — we'll create your private class space and you can keep going.
Add a pupil
The pupil will join your class straight away. You can add their guardians and ASN details next on the Team tab.
Add parent or guardian
Every guardian listed here will be asked for consent if you ever hand this pupil over to a new teacher.
Hand over this pupil to a new teacher
This will email every guardian for confirmation, and email the new teacher to accept. The handover only completes when everyone agrees. You'll lose access to this pupil the moment that happens. You can cancel before then.
